# ArchSpine Architectural Rules & Conventions

## Purpose
This document defines the architectural rules and conventions that govern the ArchSpine mirror system, ensuring consistency and coherence across the project's structure and component interactions.

## Context & Audience
Intended for system architects, developers, and maintainers who need to understand and enforce the architectural boundaries and design principles of the ArchSpine system.

## Key Responsibilities
- Establishing architectural guidelines and constraints
- Defining system structure and component relationships
- Setting maintenance boundaries for architectural decisions

## Out of Scope
- Implementation details or code-level specifications
- Operational or deployment procedures

## Key Takeaways
- Establishes the core architectural framework for the mirror system
- Defines responsibilities and boundaries for system components
- Provides guidelines for maintaining architectural integrity
