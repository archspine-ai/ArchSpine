# ArchSpine Mirror System: Vision & Orchestration

## Why This Document Exists

This document is the foundational narrative for the ArchSpine mirror system. It exists to articulate the core vision and the orchestration logic that binds all modules together. Its primary purpose is to align every contributor—human or AI agent—on the architectural intent and systemic behavior of the project. By reading this document, you will understand the "why" behind every module design decision and the structured patterns that govern inter-module communication.

## Who Should Read This

- **System architects** who need to ensure the overall design remains coherent.
- **Developers** implementing or extending any ArchSpine module.
- **Documentation maintainers** who must keep the architectural narrative consistent.
- **AI agents** that require a conceptual understanding of how modules interact and what the system aims to achieve.

This document is a stable reference point for anyone involved in extending or maintaining the mirror architecture.

## Key Takeaways

- The document defines the overarching vision that drives all module design decisions.
- Module orchestration is described as a structured pattern of interaction, not a fixed implementation.
- The narrative is meant to remain stable over time, serving as a touchstone for architectural consistency.

## What This Document Anchors

- **Architectural decisions:** Every module's purpose and interaction pattern is derived from this vision.
- **Workflows:** The orchestration patterns described here guide how modules communicate and coordinate.
- **Future extensions:** Any new module or feature must align with the vision and orchestration logic defined in this document.

## What Is Out of Scope

- Detailed implementation specifications or code-level instructions.
- Deployment or operational runbooks.
